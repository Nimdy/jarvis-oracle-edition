JARVIS · IDENTITY & PERCEPTION

dashboardV2 Who JARVIS thinks it is talking to right now (live voice+face fusion, fail-CLOSED) versus who it durably knows (persistent biometric registry) — kept strictly apart. ——

Fused Identity — Live

The fusion verdict right now. With no active voice or face signal it must read no active signals, never "0% confidence" — absence is not a low score. The last-known name is a fading snapshot, not the current resolution.

Voice & Face Signals

The two raw biometric streams feeding fusion. A signal older than its staleness floor (voice 30s, face 90s) is marked stale and must not count as live evidence.

Persistent Candidate Registry

Durable identity, disk-backed, independent of live recognition. The score is a cumulative evidence weight (promotion threshold 1.5) — not a 0–1 confidence, so it is never drawn as a percent.

Biometric Enrollment

Enrolled samples and lifetime match counts. Counts are production recognition events, not a model-accuracy score — biometric quality is unverified without a live test.

Sensor Health & Scene Perception

The Pi5 senses node and the scene tracker. STANDBY is expected while no user is present. Scene perception describes surfaces and objects — it is never used to infer who the user is; that is fusion's job alone.
