Five Conversation Modes

Rotate these — each feeds different subsystems. Corrections are worth ~10× affirmations.

1 · Grounding

Factual, correctable ("you use sqlite-vec"). Feeds capability-gate + corrections. Highest-value: catches confabulation.

2 · Autobiographical

Stories, opinions, preferences. Rich tags + emotion → fractal-recall associations.

3 · Probing

"Explain your memory system." Forces grounded self-report; trains honest introspection.

4 · Curiosity / Research

Open questions → autonomy pipeline → external_source memories linked to your interests.

5 · Emotional / Reflective

Genuine feeling → emotion classifier + personality evolution. The dead channel that needs feeding.
