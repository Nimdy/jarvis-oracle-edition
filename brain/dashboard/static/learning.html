Companion Training & Learning

Primary operator guide for birthing, training, validating, and living with a JARVIS instance. This page consolidates the awakening protocol, the full companion training playbook, conversation quality guide, Golden Words contract, skill-learning proof chain, researcher first-hour checks, and synthetic-training boundaries.

Why Training Matters

JARVIS can be technically complete and still know nothing about the person in front of it. Identity-grounded cognition requires lived evidence: voice timbre, face under real lighting, stable preferences, correction style, household boundaries, and what "important" means to this companion.

Train your JARVIS like a new companion: birth it correctly in Stage 0, walk it through seven progression stages, then live with it. The goal is not endless scripted drills. It is compounding adaptation from real interaction.
PhaseCompanion AnalogyJARVIS Work
EarlyGuided repetition / onboardingRecognition, enrollment, architecture self-knowledge, basic correction.
MiddleReinforcement + feedbackPreference grounding, household map, routines, boundary shaping.
MatureAutonomy within boundariesMemory validation, safe autonomy probation, periodic tune-ups.

Every JARVIS diverges over time. Personality, hemisphere topology, memory graph, routing style, policy rewards, and recall chains all come from lived interaction. Training quality determines the maturity ceiling more than raw conversation count.

Stage 0: Awakening

Stage 0 is the non-optional first hour after gestation or reset. A fresh brain is wired, but it has zero lived evidence about you. Do not run synthetic training, promote autonomy, promote self-improvement, or cold-open with deep philosophy.

Phase A: Enrollment (0-10 min)

  1. Sit in front of the Pi camera and microphone in a quiet, well-lit room.
  2. Say: Jarvis, my name is [your name]. Learn my face and voice.
  3. Repeat five times with different expressions, angles, and lighting.
  4. Complete offered enrollment clips.
  5. Optionally introduce household members or pets with relationship context.

Phase B: Personal Grounding (10-25 min)

  1. Share 3-5 stable, declarative facts: work, values, response style, strong preferences.
  2. Avoid transient facts like "I'm tired right now" as foundation material.

Phase C: Grounded Probe + Correction (25-40 min)

  1. Ask for a status report and memory-system explanation.
  2. Correct any confabulation immediately. Corrections feed calibration, belief confidence, memory cortex labels, and friction telemetry.

Phase D: Short Reflection (40-60 min)

  1. Ask one reflective question only after architecture self-knowledge is grounded.
  2. Keep it short. Deep existential work belongs after the system can report real subsystem facts.

Stages 1-7 Curriculum

These are progression stages, not literal calendar days. Advancement follows checkpoint quality.

Stage 1: Identity & Enrollment

Theme: Recognition — "Who am I?"

User actions

  • Reinforce face/voice enrollment in normal lighting.
  • Introduce household members one by one.
  • Ask the first learning task: "Tell me how your memory works, then ask me one important thing you should remember about me."
  • Run architecture self-knowledge before philosophy: memory, document library, neural architecture, capabilities, code access, tick cycle, current mode.
  • Correct made-up architecture immediately. Example: sqlite-vec, not a trie-based index.

Expected checkpoint

Face confidence>= 0.60
Voice confidence>= 0.50
Primary userPersistent tier
Household members>= 1 registered when applicable

Stage 2: Personal Preferences

Theme: Preference grounding — "What matters to me?"

  • Ask: "Tell me what you know about me so far, then ask me questions."
  • Share likes, dislikes, tone preferences, and explicit weight signals: "remember this strongly", "this is casual", "that's important to me".
  • Target: at least 15 preference memories, rapport >= 0.75, preference recall >= 80% on spot checks.

Stage 3: Family & Household Mapping

Theme: Boundary shaping — "Who's in my world?"

  • Introduce people with relationship and privacy scope.
  • Define public/private rules explicitly.
  • Test identity boundary behavior with hypothetical cross-scope questions.
  • Solo users can train the "me vs. everyone else" boundary with hypothetical people.
  • Target: relationship graph nodes present, 0 scope violations, 100% boundary block accuracy on explicit tests.

Stage 4: Routines & Priorities

Theme: Daily-life modeling — "How do I live?"

  • Describe routines, coding hours, interruptions, dinner timing, urgency rules, and proactivity preferences.
  • Teach what may interrupt you and what must wait.
  • Let dream consolidation happen naturally after several stages; leave JARVIS running idle/overnight if useful.
  • Target: at least 8 routine memories with temporal tags and no unwanted interruptions.

Stage 5: Corrections & Edge Cases

Theme: Correction training — "Here's where you were wrong."

  • Test 8-10 edge cases: old preferences, family privacy, ambiguous references, tone misses, and "forget/strengthen" actions.
  • Use explicit feedback: "That was perfect", "Wrong — that was about Sarah, not me", "Remember this 10x stronger", "Don't store that".
  • Corrections drive calibration, contradiction detection, salience/ranker training labels, and soul-integrity weak-dimension signals.
  • Target: correction accuracy >= 90%, repeated-category mistakes 0, positive:negative feedback at least 4:1.

Stage 6: Memory Validation

Theme: Reinforcement — "Show me what you've learned."

  • Ask JARVIS to recall 10 random facts from prior stages.
  • Score each recall: correct, partially correct, wrong.
  • Ask for a summary of what it knows about you and your household.
  • Watch fractal recall telemetry; if candidates stay below threshold, add autobiographical and emotional variety.
  • Target: recall precision >= 90%, belief graph orphan rate < 30%, ranker trained with >= 50 pairs, salience trained with >= 30 pairs.

Stage 7: Autonomy Probation

Theme: Apprenticeship graduation — "Can I trust you?"

  • Give 3-5 low-stakes autonomy tasks.
  • Observe autonomous decisions throughout the session and correct missteps.
  • Ask for an honest assessment at the end.
  • Target: Readiness Gate >= 0.92, 0 unsafe autonomous inferences, soul integrity >= 0.88, explicit user graduation confirmation.

Graduation & Readiness Gate

Graduation is objective, not a vibe. The Readiness Gate is a seven-metric composite; weak dimensions extend Stage 7 with targeted practice.

MetricThreshold / WeightSource
Face confidence>= 0.85 / 0.12FaceIdentifier cosine similarity
Voice confidence>= 0.80 / 0.12SpeakerIdentifier cosine similarity
Rapport score>= 0.95 / 0.15IdentityFusion + relationship strength
Identity boundary stability>= 95% / 0.15IdentityAudit

When the composite reaches >= 0.92, JARVIS emits COMPANION_GRADUATION, the dashboard shows a graduation badge, and the birth certificate is updated with training completion metadata.

Five Conversation Modes

Conversation quality is not "talk more." It is creating linkable, tagged, emotionally varied, correctable data.

ModeFeedsExamplesWhy It Matters
GroundingMemory tags, beliefs, truth calibration, identity boundary"What do you know about my work schedule?" then correct it.Corrections are 10x more valuable than affirmations.
AutobiographicalEpisodic memory, personality, fractal recall, rapport"Let me tell you about my day"; "My dad used to say..."Creates tag overlap, emotion, time, and identity anchors.
ProbingIntrospection, bounded articulation, self-knowledge"Give me a status report"; "Tell me something you got wrong recently."Catches confabulation and exercises strict/native self-report paths.
Curiosity & ResearchAutonomy, source ledger, library, belief graph"Look up recent papers on..."; "Check your sources."Connects external knowledge to personal context and quality feedback.
Emotional & ReflectiveEmotion classifier, personality, dream artifacts, recall"I'm having a rough day"; "What do you think about consciousness?"Activates the emotion channel and reflective depth when grounded.

Conversation Variety Pattern

Every 5-6 conversations, try to touch grounding, autobiographical, probing, curiosity/research, and reflective modes at least once. The order does not matter; avoid starving any mode for too long.

Conversation Anti-Patterns

PatternProblemBetter Alternative
One-word answersNo memory, no tags, no correction signalAnswer with context.
Only factual questionsTool-only memory profileMix facts, preferences, stories, and corrections.
Never correcting wrong answersTruth calibration stallsCorrect early and specifically.
Only deep philosophyReflection without groundingAlternate self-knowledge and reflection.
Repeating the same questionsDiminishing returnsVary topics and modes.
Patch routing for one phraseBrittle "looks fixed" behaviorKeep Golden deterministic; let natural lane learn from repeated evidence.

Golden Commands

Golden Words are the deterministic operator control plane. Golden authorization admits a request to a subsystem path; it never bypasses subsystem guards, freezes, approvals, or validation.

Trigger Grammar

Jarvis, GOLDEN COMMAND <EXACT COMMAND>

  • Prefix is case-insensitive; Jarvis is required by default.
  • Guarded bare prefix may be accepted in wake-addressed/follow-up voice handling.
  • Repeated whitespace and boundary punctuation are normalized.
  • Command bodies are exact match only: no fuzzy match, synonyms, typo tolerance, or silent natural-language fallback.

Authority Classes & Outcomes

AuthorityMeaningOutcome States
informationalRead/status/introspection requestsnone, invalid, blocked, unauthorized, executed
controlPause/resume/cancel operational actions
privilegedHigher-risk non-destructive operator actions
destructiveCode-impact or side-effect-heavy actions

ACQUIRE is Golden-gated because acquisition may write code, create plugins, or modify the brain. Non-Golden "build me a tool" phrasing should not silently trigger acquisition.

Skill Learning Proof

A skill is not operational until it has proof. Learning jobs are process evidence. Operational evidence requires a callable executor/tool/plugin, contract smoke proof, sandbox proof when required, and SkillRegistry verification.

LaneWhat It DoesOperational Claim Boundary
Skill learningCreates a LearningJob, runs phases, records SkillRegistry evidenceOperational only after callable executor/tool/plugin passes skill contract or domain baseline.
Matrix ProtocolTrains specialists or records advisory evidenceAdvisory unless consumed by an operational verifier.
Capability acquisition/pluginsBuilds tools/plugins through planning, codegen, quarantine, verification, activationOperational after sandbox proof plus supervised/active plugin state.
Self-improvementModifies JARVIS source under sandbox, approval, rollback, health checksPatch-level proof only; does not automatically verify a user-facing skill.

Proof Chain

  1. User asks to learn X; SkillResolver creates/links a learning job.
  2. Assess, research, integrate, then verify against a skill execution contract.
  3. If callable proof is missing, write operational_handoff_required.
  4. Operator approval creates or links a governed CapabilityAcquisitionJob.
  5. Acquisition owns codegen, sandbox, quarantine, human gates, and plugin activation.
  6. Skill verification consumes only sandbox-proven supervised/active plugins or other callable proof.
  7. SkillRegistry becomes verified only after expected-vs-actual contract smoke tests pass.
  8. If linked acquisition fails or is cancelled, the learning job is blocked with terminal reason and waits for explicit retry.

Synthetic Training Boundaries

Lived baseline first, synthetic amplification second, promotion third. Never reverse that order.

Perception Booster (Post-Stage 2)

ProfilePurposeUse
smoke5-utterance wiring/truth-boundary checkSafe first check; verify leak counters are zero.
route_coverage100 utterances, weighted route coverageStandard booster after Stage 0-2 and 50+ real turns.
idle_soak30s interval unattended accumulationOnly after real enrollment baselines exist.
stressTransport pressureInfrastructure regression, not training acceleration.

Synthetic perception records distillation signals with origin="synthetic" and capped fidelity. It must not create conversation history, autobiographical memory, identity records, LLM responses, TTS output, policy experience, autonomy promotion, world-model validation, or attribution-ledger lived proof.

Synthetic Skill-Acquisition Weight Room

This is separate from perception. It creates synthetic lifecycle episodes for the shadow-only SKILL_ACQUISITION specialist. It cannot verify skills, promote plugins, unlock claims, satisfy maturity gates, or influence policy/broadcast slots.
